Pizza Dough Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1c hot water
  • 1 tbl yeast
  • 2 tsp sugar

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbl olive oil
  • 2.5 c flour

Directions:

  1. Mix yeast, water, and sugar. Let stand.
  2. In mixing bowl, mix flour, oil, and salt.
  3. Once yeast mixture has risen, mix all ingredients.
  4. Let dough rest in bowl covered with a towel in a warm place for 30 minutes.
  5. Roll out dough and add sauce and toppings. Bake at 400 F for 20 minutes.
